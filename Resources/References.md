## 🏗️ Technical Concepts

### **Data Engineering & Sourcing**
* **API Integration:** Extracting structured data via REST APIs (e.g., Nominatim, OpenStreetMap).
* **Web Scraping:** Advanced extraction from HTML, structured PDFs, and geographic maps.
* **Automation:** Building resilient data pipelines using scripts and scheduled workflows (Cron/GitHub Actions).

### **Analysis & Modeling**
* **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing:** Mastering the "janitorial" phase of data science to ensure GIGO (Garbage In, Garbage Out) is avoided.
* **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Identifying patterns, distributions, and anomalies in raw datasets.
* **Statistical Relationships:** Performing Correlation Analysis and Regression to understand variable dependencies.
* **Time Series Analysis:** Visualizing and forecasting trends across temporal data.

### **Communication & Strategy**
* **Data Storytelling:** Transforming cold numbers into compelling human narratives.
* **Narrative Building:** Combining text, visuals, and interactive elements to drive decision-making.
